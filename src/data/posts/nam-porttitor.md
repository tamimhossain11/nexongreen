When a business decides to build a website, one of the first questions is: **WordPress or custom development?** Both are excellent options — but for very different use cases. Let's break it down.

## WordPress: Fast, Flexible, and Affordable

WordPress powers over 43% of all websites on the internet. It's the go-to choice for blogs, business websites, portfolios, news sites, and even e-commerce stores (via WooCommerce). Key advantages:

- **Quick launch** — a professional site can be live in days
- **Huge plugin ecosystem** for SEO, forms, e-commerce, and more
- **Easy content management** — no technical skills needed to update pages
- **Lower initial cost** compared to fully custom builds

## Custom Development: Full Control and Scalability

Custom-built websites are ideal when your project has unique requirements that off-the-shelf solutions can't meet. This includes complex web applications, custom workflows, high-performance platforms, or deeply integrated systems. Benefits include:

- **Tailor-made features** built exactly to your specifications
- **Superior performance** without plugin bloat
- **Better long-term scalability** for growing platforms

## Our Recommendation

For most small to medium businesses, **WordPress is the smartest starting point** — it's cost-effective and gets you live fast. If you're building a platform with complex logic or large-scale user needs, custom development is worth the investment.
