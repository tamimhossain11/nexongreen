You've probably heard "don't judge a book by its cover" — but when it comes to websites, users do exactly that. Research shows that **75% of people judge a company's credibility based on its website design**, and first impressions are formed in just 0.05 seconds. Bad design doesn't just look unprofessional — it costs you money.

## The Numbers Don't Lie

- A **1-second delay** in page load time can reduce conversions by 7%
- **38% of users** will stop engaging with a website if the layout is unattractive
- Companies with strong UX design see **up to 400% higher conversion rates**
- Mobile-responsive websites rank higher on Google, driving more organic traffic

## What Good Web Design Actually Means

Good design isn't just about aesthetics — it's about making it effortless for visitors to find what they need, trust your brand, and take action. This includes:

- **Clear navigation** — users shouldn't have to think about where to click
- **Fast loading times** — especially on mobile networks
- **Consistent branding** — colors, fonts, and tone that build recognition
- **Strategic CTAs** — guiding visitors toward conversion at the right moments
- **Accessibility** — ensuring your site works for all users
