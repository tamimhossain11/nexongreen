Bangladesh is no longer just an observer in the global technology arena — it's actively competing. Robotics teams representing Dreams of Bangladesh have been making waves in international competitions, building autonomous systems that rival the best in the world.

## RoboBoat 2026: DoB Joljan

DoB Joljan competed in RoboBoat 2026, an international autonomous surface vehicle (ASV) competition. The team designed, developed, and deployed a robotic boat capable of navigating complex water-based challenges using computer vision, advanced sensors, and intelligent navigation algorithms — all without human intervention.

## RoboSub 2025: DoB Hydrojan

DoB Hydrojan took on the challenge of underwater autonomy at RoboSub 2025 in the United States, one of the most prestigious autonomous underwater vehicle (AUV) competitions in the world. Their work advances Bangladesh's presence in marine robotics and deep-sea technology research.
