Digital forensics is the practice of collecting, preserving, and analyzing digital evidence from computers, networks, and storage devices. As cyber threats become more sophisticated, organizations are increasingly turning to professional forensic tools and experts to respond to incidents and investigate breaches.

## What Is FTK (Forensic Tool Kit)?

FTK, developed by Exterro, is one of the industry's most trusted digital forensics platforms. It allows investigators to:

- **Image hard drives and storage media** without altering original data
- **Recover deleted files** and fragments from disk
- **Search and index massive volumes** of data quickly
- **Analyze email, documents, and registry data** for evidence
- **Generate court-admissible reports** with full chain-of-custody documentation

## Real-World Use Cases

FTK is used in corporate investigations (data theft, fraud, policy violations), law enforcement operations, incident response after ransomware attacks, and compliance audits. It's the tool of choice when accuracy and admissibility are non-negotiable.
